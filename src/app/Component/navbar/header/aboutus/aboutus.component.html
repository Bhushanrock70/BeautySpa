Rupshrungar- Creating Experiences with Businesses

Creating a Beautiful World

Rupshrungar was conceived with the idea of the modern Indian. Founded by Maya Dhengale, whose knowledge of the international beauty industry pioneered a new era in the hair and beauty care industry in India, Naturals has made a path-breaking change in the way the beauty industry has been perceived.

So in the early 2000s, she made that dream a reality by setting up her first salon in Wani. It took her only a while to understand the market potential and the ever-increasing demand for professional grooming. since then, the Naturals team has been able to meet every challenge in this competitive industry.
